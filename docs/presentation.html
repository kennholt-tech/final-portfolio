Featured Research Project

Streaming Service Loyalty Research

This project examines consumer loyalty and cancellation risk among streaming service users.

Main research question:
What factors are associated with consumer loyalty and cancellation risk for streaming services?

Final Hypotheses

  • H1: Customers with higher loyalty index scores will show lower cancellation risk.
  • H2: Net Promoter Score category is related to customer loyalty.
  • H3: Past cancellation behavior is related to current cancellation risk.
  • H4: Respondents who cancelled a streaming service within the past year will have different loyalty levels than respondents who have not cancelled.

Dashboard Development

I built a Quarto dashboard using survey data from the Streaming Service Loyalty project.

The dashboard summarizes:

  • Valid survey responses
  • Mean loyalty index
  • High cancellation risk
  • Past-year cancellation behavior
  • NPS categories
  • Loyalty statement averages

Why the Dashboard Matters

The dashboard turns survey data into a clearer decision-making tool.

Instead of only reading raw responses, the audience can quickly understand:

  • How loyal respondents appear to be
  • Whether cancellation risk is present
  • Which loyalty statements score higher or lower
  • How cancellation history relates to loyalty
